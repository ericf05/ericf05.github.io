U.S. Health Equity Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard exploring healthcare access, income, and life expectancy across counties in the United States.

  • Built an interactive Tableau Story using county-level health and demographic datasets.
  • Created geospatial maps, scatter plots, heatmaps, and trend analyses to visualize regional health disparities.
  • Analyzed relationships between uninsured rates, physician access, income, and life expectancy.
  • Applied Tableau Level of Detail (LOD) calculations to analyze county-level health disparities and identify a nearly 20-year gap in life expectancy across U.S. counties.

Sentiment Analysis

A large-scale analysis exploring emotional patterns in COVID-19 tweets across time and regions.

  • Processed over 20 million COVID-19–related tweets to extract emotional signals.
  • Created geospatial and emoji-based visualizations to reveal regional differences.
  • Built LSTM models for emotion classification and analyzed temporal lag effects.
  • Used OLS regression to measure how major events influenced public emotional response.
